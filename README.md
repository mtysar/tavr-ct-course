# Reading a TAVR CT — Step by Step

A bite-sized, mobile-first learning app: how to read a pre-TAVR planning CT, one concept per page, with practical **3mensio** tips and quick quizzes.

Covers: the virtual annulus · getting the plane right in 3mensio · area vs perimeter sizing · coronary-obstruction risk (height / sinus / STJ / VTC) · calcium · fluoroscopy deployment angles (coplanar / cusp-overlap / Rule of 22) · iliofemoral access · reporting.

**Sources:** SCCT Expert Consensus on CT for TAVR (Blanke et al., 2019) plus verified 3mensio workflow research.

> Educational tool for training. Always confirm every measurement against the raw CT, your local 3mensio version, and the heart team. Not medical advice.

## View it
Open `index.html` in any browser — it's a single self-contained file (works offline). Progress is saved on your device.
